LEARNINGS OF INTRO TO COMPUTING

Chapter 1: Introduction to Computing

In this chapter, I delved into the history of computing, recalling significant events and inventions that shaped the field. I also known the names of inventors alongside their groundbreaking inventions.

Chapter 2: Professions & Careers in Computing

This chapter focused on identifying various IT professions and their roles in the computing industry. I gained an understanding of the skills necessary to thrive as an IT professional.

Chapters 3 and 4: Information Processing Cycle

I grasped the sequential order of events involved in processing information, encompassing input, processing, storage, and output.

Chapter 5: Basic Concepts of Computer Security

This chapter introduced the crucial concepts of data confidentiality, integrity, and availability. It also explored security issues specific to operating systems, networks, and database systems.

Chapter 6: The Internet & World Wide Web

Delving into the history of the Internet, I learned about its components and how standards, services, and protocols facilitate the interoperability of different hardware and software. I gained a basic understanding of Internet architecture and addressing. Additionally, I explored the workings of the World Wide Web, web browsers, and web servers.

Chapter 7: Basic HTML & CSS

This chapter on Basic HTML & CSS provided me with a unique blend of fascination and challenge. Exploring how to structure text and image content for the web using HTML added a layer of depth to my understanding of web development. Simultaneously, delving into the art of styling web pages using Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) also proved to be both captivating and challenging as well for me. Balancing the creative aspect of design with the intricacies of coding made this lesson a compelling journey, leaving me with a sense of accomplishment and excitement for the possibilities within web development.
