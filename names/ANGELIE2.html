LEARNINGS OF INTRO TO COMPUTING

Chapter 1: Introduction to Computing

In this first chapter, I explore how computers came to be. I learn about important events and clever ideas that shaped the field. I also get to know the smart people who made significant contributions.

Chapter 2: Professions & Careers in Computing

This chapter introduces me to the many different jobs in the computer world. I learn about the important roles each job plays in making computers better. It helps me understand the skills needed for a good computer job.

Chapters 3 and 4: Information Processing Cycle

In these chapters, I learn how computers work with information. I understand the steps they take, like putting in data, doing operations, saving info, and getting it back. It helps me understand how computers change data.

Chapter 5: Basic Concepts of Computer Security

This part talks about how I can keep my computer stuff secure. I learn about important ideas like keeping info private, making sure it's right, and having it when I need it. also see the issues with computer safety, like protecting my main computer, connecting with other things.

Chapter 6: The Internet & World Wide Web

In this chapter, I dive into the story of the Internet. I find out about its parts and the rules, services, and ways computers work together. I understand the basics of the Internet and how addresses work. I also learn about the Web, the software for moving around, and the strong computers that keep web pages.

Chapter 7: Basic HTML & CSS

In this lesson, I learn how to organize stuff for websites using HTML5. I get to know HTML and get a simple idea of making web pages look better with Cascading Style Sheets (CSS).
