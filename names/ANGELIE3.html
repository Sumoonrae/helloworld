Yet, a bittersweet tinge lingers, whispering of goodbyes to familiar faces, comfortable routines, and the sheltered haven that fostered my growth. Fear, a masked guest, l urks at the edges, whispering about the uncertainties that lie ahead. But amidst this maelstrom of emotions, one feeling reigns supreme: gratitude. Gratitude for the opportunities that brought me here, for the mentors who guided my path, and for the friends who became my family. It is this gratitude that fuels my resolve, pushing me to embrace the future with open arms, knowing that even though the journey ahead is paved with unknowns, it is a journey I will embark on with courage, determination, and the unwavering support of those who have helped me reach this milestone.
